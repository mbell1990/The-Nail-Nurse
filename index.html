Builder in a Bottle

Builder in a Bottle (BIAB) is a nail-strengthening gel polish that's applied as an overlay on natural nails. It's similar to regular gel polish but has a thicker consistency that dries into a stronger, more durable layer. BIAB can help nails grow steadily and lessens the chances of snapping or breakage.


Recommended for weak or brittle nails, nail biters, and clients who struggle to grow their nails. BIAB can be left nude or with gel colour.

Gel Manicure & Gel Extensions

A gel manicure is a cosmetic nail treatment that uses a special gel polish that's cured with a UV or LED lamp to create a shiny, long-lasting manicure.Gel manicures can last for up to four weeks and are more durable and hard-wearing than regular nail polish. They can also be used to strengthen natural nails or lengthen nails with tips.


Gel extensions are a type of nail enhancement that can make nails look longer and stronger, and are similar to acrylic nails.
